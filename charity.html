All are requested to cooperate wholeheartedly in the ongoing and the intended activities of the Human Rights Organization.
It has been decided to found a Charity Home (Karunya Bhavan), locating Trivandrum Medical College, adjacent to the Regional Cancer Centre and SAT Hospital, for offering free food and accommodation to those poor cancer, brain tumour, stroke and heart block patients coming from far off places for taking treatment at Trivandrum Regional Cancer Centre and Sree Chithra Hospital. It is expected to cost around Rs 10 crore to establish this charity home. It is requested that this project be made a great success and help the needy patients. It has been decided to institute a Medical Aid Fund, in collaboration with SAT Hospital, Trivandrum, for the treatment of children suffering from Type I Diabetes, who are below 18 years old. Considering the state of affairs of lacking wheelchairs in Taluk Hospitals in Kerala, it has also been decided to distribute wheelchairs. We request the cooperation and special prayers of all of you in such great acts of charity.

Charity Activities planned by the Organization
1) With the slogan "With Might in the Field of Charity....", it has been decided to found a Charity
Home (Karunya Bhavan), locating at Trivandrum Medical College, to provide free food and
accommodation for about a hundred patients coming from far off places to Regional Cancer Centre
and Sree Chitra Medical Centre in Trivandrum, Kerala, who are financially poor, for treatment of
deadly diseases like cancer, as a helping hand of solace. Everyone is requested to cooperate to make
this great venture a success. This will be started soon.
2) It has been decided to institute a Medical Aid Fund, in collaboration with Sree Avittom Thirunal
(SAT) Hospital, Trivandrum in Kerala, for the treatment of children suffering from Type I Diabetes, who are below 18 years old. All are requested to cooperate with this venture and make it successful.

A small helping hand to the Emergency Relief Fund to provide medical assistance and relief to those suffering under the Covid epidemic.

It's a chance to do good and to act well. The country is suffering from the Covid pandemic. Millions of people are getting sick, thousands of people are dying, our brothers are dying without oxygen and struggling to sustain their lives. Let's join our hands with the government health systems in Kerala as a precautionary measure to prevent the spread of the disease and to save the lives of the sick, irrespective of any differences in caste, creed, religion, and political ideologies. To save the dying people in India, the big-hearted rulers of the Gulf countries, sent oxygen cylinders to our country. They have shown us their solidarity. It is not enough however big we praise their good minds. May God bless them. Let's support the work of the government. Let's also join our hands with our government for the benefit of the general people. Let's extend our help, no matter however small it is. We need to understand the plight of our brothers who died on the streets in Kerala and other states. Thousands of people are starving for oxygen - husband in front of his wife, children in front of their parents. It's a situation where no one can do anything. Our country is going through a major crisis. We must stand together to overcome this crisis in Kerala as well. the Human Rights Organisation are working together with the government voluntarily. Let's support the good work of the government and their efforts to save the lives of the people. Let's join hands with the Government of Kerala for providing emergency facilities for the treatment such as oxygen cylinders, masks, and PPE kits, for equipping hospital facilities in schools, for purchase of other necessary items including beds, and the provision of medical facilities and emergency services by the Government of Kerala. To save the people of Kerala from this epidemic, the donations you make, however small they are, are absolutely huge assets. It also provides support and a shield for many activities that are intended to be carried out in the community. We are acting as a helping hand for those suffering under the covid pandemic and also acting as shield and cover for cancer patients.
We are requesting and warmly welcoming everyone to work together to protect human rights and humanity and for the betterment of this country, regardless of caste, creed, or political affiliation.
